2021 Intellectual Property Policy (IPP) of the Southern Luzon State University

Request for E-Copy of the 2021 Intellectual Property Policy (IPP) of the Southern Luzon State University (SLSU)

Welcome to the official request form for an electronic copy of the 2021 Intellectual Property Policy (IPP) of Southern Luzon State University (SLSU). This document outlines our policies and procedures regarding the management, protection, and commercialization of intellectual property developed at SLSU.

Purpose of this Form:

This form is intended to facilitate secured and controlled access to the 2021 IPP of the SLSU, ensuring compliance with university policies and intellectual property rights.

Eligibility for Request:

  • Faculty and staff of SLSU
  • Students of SLSU
  • Research partners and collaborators
  • External stakeholders involved in SLSU projects

How to Request:

  1. Fill out all required fields. For verification of your affiliation and purpose for accessing the IPP.
  2. Submit the completed form. Your request will be reviewed promptly, and eligibility will be confirmed.
  3. Receive the IPP securely. If your request is approved, the IPP will be sent to you via an email link.

Confidentiality and Data Protection:

  1. We are committed to protecting your personal information and will handle your data in strict accordance with the Republic Act 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012) and university policies.
  2. We are committed to protecting your data in accordance with applicable privacy laws and university policies.

Need Assistance?

  1. If you have any questions or need further help, please don't hesitate to contact the SLSU Innovation and Technology Support Services Office at itsso@slsu.edu.ph or call (042) 540 1115.

Thank you for your cooperation and commitment to respecting intellectual property rights at SLSU! We are here to help you understand and navigate our policies.
